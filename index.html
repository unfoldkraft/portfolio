Bridge Design Concept

Conceptual Bridge Design Competition over the Limmat in the city of Zurich, Switzerland. Featuring a compact composite superstructure with S335 Steel and UHPC top deck. The substructure utilizes two integral concrete supports that anchor into pile foundations under the river, where abutments accommodate expansion and contraction from temperature changes.

Materiality: Steel + UHPC

Dimensions: Span 40m

Solar Decathlon Design Competition

Description: US Department of Energy Solar Decathlon Grand Winner 2022 - New York Harbor School Expansion & Renovation.

Background: This project to expand the New York Harbor School on Governor’s Island focuses on enhancing its unique location and maritime-focused education. The proposal creates new community space, circulation, and connection to the campus through sculpted landscapes, public volumes, and paths. We reach a net-zero design by integrating photovoltaic panels, mindful reuse of construction material, a green roof, geothermal energy piles, a rainwater capture system, and other technologies. Engineering design flood levels and climate conditions were considered through design in section, plant selection, and structural innovations such as the bathtub-style foundation to create a resilient campus.

Advisors: Melody Baglione, Nader Tehrani, Pamela Cabrera, Julian Palacio

Collaborators: Ji Yong Chung, Shannagh Crowe, Tate Liang, Sophia Zhao, Benny Wu, Larry Zeng, Sarah Coraizaca, Samantha Wu, Mizanul Hoque, Amelia Roopnarine
