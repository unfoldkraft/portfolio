Projects

Bicycle Cello Stand

The Bicycle Cello Stand is a custom-designed stand that securely holds a cello while integrating bicycle frame components. This innovative design allows for a sturdy and portable solution for musicians needing a reliable support system for their instrument. The structure is reinforced with adjustable clamps and cushioned supports to ensure the safety of the cello.

Modular Milk Crate Bike Rack System

This bike rack system is designed for versatile cargo transport using modular milk crates. The system allows for easy attachment and removal of crates, making it perfect for carrying groceries, gear, and other essentials. Its sturdy metal frame ensures durability, while the modular aspect enables users to customize their carrying capacity based on their needs.
